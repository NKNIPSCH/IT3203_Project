Replace Google Search

Google tracks every search you make. Try these private alternatives that don't track you or create a profile about you.

Why Switch from Google Search?

Google Search is great at finding things, but it comes at a cost - your privacy. Google keeps track of:

  • Everything you search for
  • Which results you click
  • Where you are when you search
  • What device you're using

It then sells this information to other companies, through their various google products, you are the commedity being sold with google.

Making the Switch Easy

Here's how to switch your default search engine:

  • In Brave: Already set up with Brave Search
  • In Firefox: Settings → Search → Choose your new search engine
  • In Chrome: Settings → Search engine → Manage search engines → Add
  • On Mobile: Install the search engine's app or change browser settings

Pro Tips

  • Try DuckDuckGo's "!bang" commands for direct searches on other sites
  • Use Startpage when you need Google-quality results but want privacy
  • Brave Search shows you what percentage of results are from their own index
  • All these options work on both computers and phones
