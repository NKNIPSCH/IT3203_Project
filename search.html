Replace Google Search

Google tracks every search you make. Try these private alternatives that don’t track or profile you.

Best Private Search Engines

  • Brave Search: Independent index, no tracking.
  • DuckDuckGo: Easy Google alternative, no search history stored.
  • Startpage: Google results without tracking.
  • Mojeek: Fully independent, privacy-first search engine.

Why Switch from Google Search?

  • Google tracks everything you search, your clicks, and even your location.
  • Search profiles are used to target ads and manipulate search rankings.
  • Private search engines don’t store your searches or personal data.
