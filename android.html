Want a phone that respects your privacy? Here are some great options to reduce Google's control over your device.

Before You Switch

Important things to remember:

  • Back up your data using Google Takeout
  • Check if your phone model is supported by your chosen system
  • Some banking apps might not work on custom Android systems
  • Keep your old system backed up in case you want to switch back
